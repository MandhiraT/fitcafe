# FitCafe Image Guidelines

## Overview
This document provides guidelines for placeholder images to be used in the FitCafe sales page. These images should be replaced with custom AI-generated images once API access is available.

## Image Requirements
- **License:** Must be free for commercial use (no attribution required preferred)
- **Quality:** High resolution (minimum 1200px width for desktop, 600px for mobile)
- **Style:** Natural, authentic, professional — avoid overly staged stock photos
- **Color Palette:** Warm tones, coffee-inspired colors (browns, creams, earthy tones)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG (optimized for web)

## Specific Images Needed

### 1. Hero Product Image
- **Description:** Premium coffee bag/package with clean design
- **Style:** Product photography on natural background (wooden table, marble, or neutral fabric)
- **Elements:** Coffee beans scattered around, steam rising from a cup nearby
- **Mood:** Premium, trustworthy, natural
- **Suggested Sources:** Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay
- **Search Terms:** "premium coffee bag", "coffee product photography", "gourmet coffee packaging"

### 2. Morning Ritual Lifestyle
- **Description:** Woman in her 30s enjoying morning coffee at home
- **Style:** Natural lighting from window, cozy home environment
- **Elements:** Peaceful expression, fitness tracker on wrist, healthy breakfast nearby, notebook or book
- **Mood:** Calm, intentional, wellness-focused
- **Suggested Sources:** Unsplash, Pexels
- **Search Terms:** "morning coffee woman", "healthy lifestyle coffee", "wellness morning routine"

### 3. Work From Home Lifestyle
- **Description:** Professional working from home with coffee
- **Style:** Clean, modern home office setup
- **Elements:** Focused expression, laptop, notebook, coffee cup on desk
- **Mood:** Productive, focused, balanced
- **Suggested Sources:** Unsplash, Pexels
- **Search Terms:** "work from home coffee", "home office professional", "remote work coffee"

### 4. Ingredients Visual
- **Description:** Flat lay of natural ingredients
- **Style:** Clean, minimalist arrangement on neutral background
- **Elements:** Green coffee beans, green tea leaves, natural supplements, fresh herbs
- **Mood:** Natural, transparent, health-focused
- **Suggested Sources:** Unsplash, Pexels
- **Search Terms:** "coffee ingredients flat lay", "natural supplements photography", "healthy ingredients arrangement"

### 5. Social Proof / Transformation
- **Description:** Authentic-looking people (diverse ages, ethnicities)
- **Style:** Candid, natural expressions (not overly posed)
- **Elements:** People looking happy/confident, casual clothing, natural settings
- **Mood:** Genuine, relatable, positive
- **Suggested Sources:** Unsplash, Pexels
- **Search Terms:** "happy diverse people", "authentic lifestyle photos", "genuine smiles"

## Technical Specifications
- **Desktop Images:** 1200px width minimum, JPG quality 80-90%
- **Mobile Images:** 600px width minimum, JPG quality 70-80%
- **File Naming:** Use descriptive names (e.g., `hero-product.jpg`, `morning-ritual.jpg`)
- **Alt Text:** Include descriptive alt text for accessibility

## Recommended Free Stock Photo Sites
- **Unsplash:** https://unsplash.com (free for commercial use, no attribution required)
- **Pexels:** https://pexels.com (free for commercial use, no attribution required)
- **Pixabay:** https://pixabay.com (free for commercial use, no attribution required)

## Future Replacement
These placeholder images will be replaced with custom AI-generated images once API access to Midjourney/FLUX/DALL-E is available. The AI-generated images will follow the same style and mood guidelines but will be unique to the FitCafe brand.
