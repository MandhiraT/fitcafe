What's Inside — And Why We Chose It

We believe in full transparency. Here's what goes into every cup of FitCafe, and what each ingredient actually does:

☕ Green Coffee Bean Extract

Coffee beans in their natural, unroasted state contain chlorogenic acid, a compound that research suggests may help support healthy glucose metabolism and assist the body in utilizing stored energy more efficiently.

🍵 Green Tea Extract

Green tea is one of the most extensively studied botanical ingredients for metabolic support. Its natural catechins work synergistically with caffeine to gently encourage calorie burning throughout the day — even at rest.

🧠 L-Theanine

This naturally occurring amino acid, found primarily in tea leaves, is known for its ability to promote calm alertness. When combined with caffeine, L-Theanine helps smooth out the energy curve — supporting focus without the jitters or afternoon crash often associated with coffee.

⚖️ Chromium Picolinate

Chromium is an essential trace mineral that plays a role in macronutrient metabolism. Research indicates it may help support healthy blood sugar levels already within normal range, which can influence feelings of hunger and between-meal cravings.

🔥 L-Carnitine

L-Carnitine functions as a transport molecule in the body, helping move fatty acids into cells' mitochondria where they can be converted into usable energy. Less stored, more burned.

🌿 Garcinia Cambogia

Derived from a tropical fruit also known as Malabar tamarind, Garcinia Cambogia contains HCA (hydroxycitric acid), which has been shown to support appetite control and help reduce the conversion of excess carbohydrates into fat.

Every ingredient is clearly listed, appropriately dosed, and manufactured in an FDA-registered, GMP-certified facility. We believe you have the right to know exactly what you're consuming.
